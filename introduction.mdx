## Overview

ForgeKit helps you maintain a tidy home with an ADHD-friendly approach. You get gentle guidance through daily tasks without overwhelming lists or guilt. Designed for neurodivergent minds, it uses calming visuals, smart automation, and rewarding feedback to make cleaning feel achievable and satisfying.

Download from Google Play today and experience a cleaner space with less stress. App Store support comes soon.

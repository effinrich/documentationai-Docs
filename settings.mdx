## Overview

ForgeKit provides a comprehensive settings menu to tailor the app to your cleaning routine and preferences. Access settings from the main menu by tapping the gear icon in the top-right corner. Customize rooms, tasks, notifications, subscriptions, and privacy options to create a calm, personalized experience.

<Callout kind="tip">
Review your settings weekly to keep tasks aligned with your current needs and maintain your streak.
</Callout>

## Room and Task Management

Manage your home's rooms and associated tasks directly in the app. Add, edit, or remove rooms to reflect your space accurately. Tasks auto-generate based on your schedule, but you can override them anytime.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Room Settings" icon="settings">
    Open the app and navigate to **Settings > Rooms & Tasks**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a Room" icon="plus">
    Tap **Add Room**, enter the name (e.g., "Living Room"), and select recurring tasks like vacuuming or dusting.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize Tasks" icon="edit">
    For each room, toggle tasks on/off or adjust frequency (daily, weekly).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes" icon="check">
    Tap **Save** to update your daily task list instantly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Setting | Description | Options |
|---------|-------------|---------|
| Room Name | Custom label for the space | Text input, up to 50 characters |
| Task Frequency | How often the task appears | Daily, Weekly, Bi-weekly, Monthly |
| Priority | Task urgency level | Low, Medium, High |

## Notification and Reminder Customization

Tailor notifications to feel supportive rather than overwhelming. Set gentle reminders that match your productive times.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Android" icon="android">
    Go to device **Settings > Apps > ForgeKit > Notifications**. Enable category-specific alerts for tasks and streaks.
    
    Customize in-app:
    
    ````bash
    # Example notification preferences (via app export)
    {
      "dailyReminder": "9:00 AM",
      "streakAlert": true,
      "completionSound": "gentle-chime"
    }
    ````
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="iOS" icon="apple">
    Use iOS **Settings > Notifications > ForgeKit**. Allow critical alerts for streak maintenance.
    
    In-app options mirror Android for consistency.
    
    ````json
    {
      "dailyReminder": "9:00 AM",
      "streakAlert": true,
      "completionVibration": "soft-pulse"
    }
    ````
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout kind="info">
Notifications work offline-first, ensuring instant feedback even without internet.
</Callout>

## Data Privacy and Pattern Learning Settings

ForgeKit prioritizes your privacy—data stays on-device by default. Enable pattern learning to adapt tasks to your habits.

<Expandable title="Advanced Privacy Options" default-open="false">

Disable pattern learning if preferred:

````json
{
  "patternLearning": false,
  "dataExport": "json",
  "shareAnalytics": false
}
````

Export settings via **Settings > Privacy > Export Data** for backup.

Key privacy features:
- No cloud sync without opt-in
- Local storage only for habits
- Delete all data anytime

</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Troubleshooting Settings">
If sync issues occur, toggle **Offline Mode** off in privacy settings and reconnect.
</Expandable>

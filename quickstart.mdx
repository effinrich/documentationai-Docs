## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" title="Before You Begin">
Make sure you have:
- An Android device running Android 8.0 or later
- A stable internet connection for initial setup
- About 5 minutes to complete onboarding
</Callout>

ForgeKit makes cleaning simple and ADHD-friendly. Follow these steps to get started quickly.

## Download and Install the App

<Tabs>
<Tab title="Android" icon="android">
Search for `ForgeKit Tidy` in the Google Play Store or use this direct link:

[Get it on Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.forgekit.tidy)

Once downloaded:
1. Open the app installer
2. Tap `Install`
3. Launch ForgeKit after installation
</Tab>
<Tab title="iOS" icon="apple" disabled>
Coming soon to the App Store.
</Tab>
</Tabs>

## Create Your Account and Onboard

<Steps>
<Step title="Open the App" icon="smartphone">
Launch ForgeKit. You'll see a calming welcome screen with soft teals and greens.
</Step>

<Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
Choose `Sign up with email` or use Google/Apple sign-in.

Enter your email: `your.email@example.com`

Set a password or authorize with your provider.

Tap `Continue`.
</Step>

<Step title="Complete Onboarding" icon="check-circle">
Swipe through the quick tour:
- Learn about daily tasks
- Set your cleaning preferences
- Enable gentle reminders

Tap `Get Started`.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
Onboarding takes under 2 minutes and introduces key features like streaks and smart scheduling.
</Callout>

## Add Your First Room

<Steps>
<Step title="Navigate to Rooms" icon="home">
From the dashboard, tap the `+` icon or go to `Rooms` in the bottom navigation.
</Step>

<Step title="Add Room Details" icon="plus">
- Room name: `Living Room`
- Frequency: `Daily` or `Weekly`
- Tasks: `Vacuum floor`, `Wipe surfaces`

Tap `Save`.
</Step>

<Step title="Review Schedule" icon="calendar">
ForgeKit auto-generates your first task: `Vacuum living room`.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Complete Your First Cleaning Task

<Steps>
<Step title="View Today's Tasks" icon="list">
On the home screen, see `Vacuum living room` listed.
</Step>

<Step title="Mark Complete" icon="check">
Tap the task. Enjoy the satisfying animation and `+10 points` reward!

Your 1-day streak begins.
</Step>

<Step title="Celebrate" icon="award">
Check your streak counter. Tap `See streak details` for motivation.
</Step>
</Steps>

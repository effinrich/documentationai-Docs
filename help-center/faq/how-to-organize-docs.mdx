## Overview

Use Folders and Pages to keep things tidy in ForgeKit.

## Steps

1. Click "New Folder" in your sidebar
2. Name the folder (e.g., "API Docs")
3. Inside the folder, click "New Page"
4. Drag and drop pages to reorder
5. Use nested folders for categories

## Tip

Folders can be collapsed for easier navigation.
